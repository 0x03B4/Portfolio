01 - Introduction

I have always been drawn to the "under the hood" side of software. I enjoy backend engineering because it feels like solving a complex math problem. It is about structure, data flow and efficiency.

Right now, I build most of my projects in the Python ecosystem (Django and Django REST Framework) because it lets me move fast. I am also a big fan of HTMX for building interactive apps without over-complicating the frontend. I am comfortable versioning with Git and managing data with SQL, PostgreSQL and working in Linux.

That said, I am not married to Python. I focus on the fundamentals, so I am happy to pick up Java, C#, Go or whatever the team uses. I pride myself on being a fast, curious, teachable and efficient learner.
